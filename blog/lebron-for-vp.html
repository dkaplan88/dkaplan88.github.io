Lebron for VP

By Dan Kaplan • July 27, 2020

This is a humorous and speculative piece suggesting LeBron James as Joe Biden's vice presidential candidate.

Why LeBron Would Be a Good VP

1. Race Relations and Progressive Politics

  • Biden struggled with race issues in debates
  • LeBron has been a long-time supporter of Black Lives Matter
  • He has demonstrated leadership in education reform

2. Addressing Biden's Weaknesses

  • Counters Biden's age (77 years old)
  • Brings an "outsider" perspective to Washington
  • Could help control media narrative
  • Potentially sway electoral votes in Ohio and Florida

What's in It for LeBron

By becoming VP, LeBron could:

  • Transcend sports in a way no athlete has before
  • Achieve his dream of creating societal change
  • Become "unmatched by any athlete"

Conclusion

While acknowledging the high risk, Biden should "go for the slam dunk, and pick Lebron James as his VP."

This piece is clearly tongue-in-cheek, using basketball metaphors and presenting a playful hypothetical scenario about LeBron's potential political career.
